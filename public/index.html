How Ranking Works

Result Prioritization Summary

Ranking blends semantic similarity with structured trait matching, then applies mode-specific boosts and penalties.

  • Closest overall match: balanced blend of caption similarity, visual description similarity, and trait matches.
  • Shape & overall form: emphasizes silhouette/component cues (integrated shell, high-back taper, base geometry) and penalizes mismatched forms.
  • Color & material details: boosts exact color and finish alignment (e.g., red, chrome/polished) and penalizes mismatches.
  • Image-led search boost: when searching from an uploaded image, exact source-image and same-product records receive priority boosts.
  • Trait sanity checks: penalties apply for clear mismatches (category/type, partial product images, and unsupported required cues).
